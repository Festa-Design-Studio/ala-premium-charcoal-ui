Premium Lump Charcoal

Àlá Premium Lump Charcoal represents the pinnacle of charcoal production, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern quality control processes. Our artisans carefully select premium hardwoods, which are then kiln-fired using time-honored methods that have been perfected over generations.

The result is a high-performance charcoal that burns hotter, cleaner, and longer than conventional products. With its exceptionally low ash production and consistent piece size, our Premium Lump Charcoal delivers reliable results for both commercial kitchens and discerning home grillers.

Production Process

Our charcoal is produced using a carefully controlled process:

  1. Select hardwoods are harvested from sustainably managed forests
  2. Wood is naturally dried to optimal moisture levels
  3. Traditional kilns are used for slow carbonization at precisely controlled temperatures
  4. Charcoal undergoes rigorous quality control testing
  5. Products are carefully sorted and packaged to minimize breakage

Quality Control

Every batch of our Premium Lump Charcoal undergoes thorough testing to ensure it meets our exacting standards for:

  • Heat output and efficiency
  • Ash content and composition
  • Moisture levels
  • Fixed carbon percentage
  • Consistent size distribution
  • Absence of contaminants

Detailed Specifications

  • Material: 100% natural hardwood (no additives or chemicals)
  • Piece Size: 5–15 cm (sorted for consistency)
  • Packaging: Durable, moisture-resistant bags (5kg, 10kg, 20kg)
  • Burn Characteristics: Low smoke, minimal sparking
  • Certifications: Sustainably sourced, compliant with export standards

Usage Guide

  1. Arrange charcoal in a pyramid for easy lighting.
  2. Use natural firelighters or kindling—avoid lighter fluid for best results.
  3. Allow charcoal to ash over before spreading for even heat.
  4. Ideal for grilling, smoking, and open-fire cooking.
  5. Dispose of ashes safely after cooling.
